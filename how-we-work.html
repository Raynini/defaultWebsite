How We Work

Clear, simple policies that keep projects smooth and predictable.

Appointment Only

All work is scheduled in advance. Small jobs are typically 1–3 days out; larger jobs include an agreed timeline before we start.

Mobile Diagnostics (25mi)

On-site diagnostics within 25 miles of Manchester, NH. Service call fee: $60 — credited toward the repair if you book.

Quotes & Deposits

We quote before we wrench. Parts-heavy or longer jobs require a deposit.

Customer-Supplied Parts

We can install parts you provide, but they’re installed at your risk (defects/fitment failures are the customer’s responsibility).

Labor Warranty

90-day labor warranty on qualifying work. Parts follow manufacturer policies.
